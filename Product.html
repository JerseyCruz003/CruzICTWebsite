Drawie Gloves

According to ScreamNPixel, an artist drawing glove is more specifically known as an “anti-fouling” glove. It is meant to cover the side of your hand that rests on a digital drawing surface. It is typically designed to cover two fingers, ring and pinky, or sometimes only one finger, the pinky. While drawing gloves remain popular for artists to reduce smudging within traditional styles of art such as graphite and charcoal, these gloves are increasingly being embraced among digital artists worldwide.
