Drawie Gloves

Why do we need to buy some drawing gloves?
It is important to keep in mind that there are several reasons why professional artists prefer these gloves. In any case, the main ones will be: You can protect the side of your hand, preventing sweat from falling on the surface where you are drawing. They reduce friction, which makes you can move your hand much more easily on any surface. In the case of tablets, these will remain cleaner and have greater durability.
